Common questions

FAQ

Is this a substitute for seeing my doctor?

No. Kurryculum consultations are educational supplement advisory sessions, not medical appointments. We do not diagnose conditions, prescribe medications or replace your primary care physician. Always consult your doctor for medical concerns.

What is the difference between a pharmacist consultation and a doctor visit?

A pharmacist (RPh) specialises in medications and supplements — their interactions, dosing, timing and compatibility with your diet. These sessions focus exclusively on supplement advisory guidance, not diagnosis or treatment.

What happens in a consultation?

We discuss your cultural diet, health goals, current medications, and any lab results you want to share. You receive written personalised supplement advisory notes with dosing guidance, timing tips, and product suggestions.

Can you review my current supplements?

Yes — reviewing your existing supplement stack for potential interactions, redundancies, or gaps is one of the most valuable parts of a session.

What format is the call?

Video call via Zoom or Google Meet. A calendar invite with the link is sent automatically when you book via Calendly.

Do you cover Indian, Pakistani, West African and Caribbean diets?

Yes — this is our specialty. We understand dal, biryani, jollof, roti, jerk chicken and the specific nutritional patterns of multicultural diets in a way that generic advice never does.

What is the cancellation policy?

Full refund if cancelled more than 24 hours before your appointment. No refund within 24 hours, but you may reschedule once.
